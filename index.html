Intermediate

Once you've mastered the basics (typically around a year), learning the advanced flat serve and volley is next. You should also be hitting your groundstrokes with topspin for less margin of error and learning the slice, which is handy when defending.
In addition, you should pick out an intermediate/advanced racket that has a head size around 97-100 square inches and a weight around 11.2oz or 11.4oz strung (typically the standard model of a racket series).

  • Never use anything new in a match
  • Stay calm and relaxed

  • Use high intensity movements
  • Work on one specific thing at a time
  • Do workouts and conditioning
  • Try shawdow swings (swing motions without the ball)
